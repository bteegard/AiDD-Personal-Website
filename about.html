About Me

My Story

Currently pursuing my MSIS degree, I am deeply engaged in exploring how information systems can transform business operations and drive strategic decision-making. The Kelley School's rigorous curriculum and industry-focused approach have provided me with invaluable insights into the intersection of technology and business.

Academic Background

My academic journey at the Indiana Kelley School of Business has been exceptional. I'm currently pursuing my Master of Science in Information Systems (MSIS) with a 3.70 GPA, while also completing my Bachelor of Science in Business with majors in Information Systems, Operations Management, and Business Analytics (3.48 GPA). I've been honored to receive a Graduate Assistant position based on academic achievement and was awarded the prestigious Miami County Remit Scholarship ($34,000) for my academic excellence and community impact.
